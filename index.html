ABOUT US

Having moved to Chinchilla as a family in 1990, Fromm’s catering started in 1999 with Lauretta, (having years of experience catering for large family gatherings) and Christle (just started as an apprentice Chef at the Dalby RSL) They saw there was a need for this business when the options for Catering in Chinchilla were limited.  There was a call for caterers willing to cater in different locations around the area with limited facilities.  Now, with 20 years of local experience catering for all types of events which have included Melon Festival lunches, Weddings, Staff Christmas Parties, High Teas, Finger food/Cocktail events, Campdraft Canteens, right through to fundraising events for the likes of Drought Angels with their annual Stockmans Stampede. 

Christle and Lauretta look forward to working with you and designing a menu to suit your function and location.
